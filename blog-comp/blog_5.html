Top Companies Hiring BSIT Graduates

If you're getting ready to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) degree, you might be wondering which companies are hiring. Here are some of the top companies that are actively seeking BSIT graduates:


1. Google

As one of the biggest tech companies in the world, Google is always on the lookout for talented IT professionals. With a BSIT degree, you could be a strong candidate for roles such as software engineer, data analyst, or cybersecurity analyst.

Microsoft

Microsoft is another tech giant that offers a wide range of job opportunities for BSIT graduates. Some of the roles you might be qualified for include software developer, systems analyst, or database administrator.

Amazon

As one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world, Amazon has a growing need for IT professionals. With a BSIT degree, you might be qualified for roles such as cloud architect, cybersecurity analyst, or software developer.

IBM

IBM has a long history of innovation in the IT industry, and they're always looking for talented professionals to join their team. With a BSIT degree, you could be a strong candidate for roles such as software engineer, systems analyst, or cybersecurity consultant.

5. Accenture

Accenture is a global consulting firm that provides a wide range of IT services to clients around the world. With a BSIT degree, you could be a good fit for roles such as IT consultant, software developer, or data analyst.

By targeting your job search to these companies and others like them, you can increase your chances of landing a rewarding and high-paying job after graduation.
